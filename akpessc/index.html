Why Join?

AKPESSC is disposed towards bringing together Power & Energy Society volunteers from all Student Branches in Kerala to foster technological innovation and its a jaunt to discover new ideas. It provides a platform for technical advancements and also helps to reminisce about the activities that tracked their journey as a volunteer and will also get them inspired to divergent thinking by meeting new people. The technical extravaganza will provide a chance to know more about Green Energy and Clean Energy and helps them in redefining goals to persuade in their field of interest.
