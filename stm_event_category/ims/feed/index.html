]]> Technical Talk on ‘Instrumentation: Scopes and Advancements’ https://cecieee.org/events/technical-talk-on-instrumentation-scopes-and-advancements/ Sat, 31 Mar 2018 16:56:53 +0000 http://cecieee.org/?post_type=stm_event&p=392818 A technical talk on the topic ‘Instrumentation: Scopes and Advancements’ was conducted on 21st
April 2017, along with the inauguration ceremony. He introduced wide arrays of technology where
instrumentation engineering has set its imprint and some which were about to be dominated.
Pointing out daily phenomena, he meticulously related what each of them had in common with
instrumentation and how one or the other would’ve become impossible without it. Then he went on
describing various possibilities and outcomes that instrumentation engineering will bring to the
world. IOT or the Internet of Things, which has become the doors to a smartly automated living for
the future world, has based itself to this field of engineering. Being an insider of various
technological societies, he gave an insight into the technological world’s path and destination.
