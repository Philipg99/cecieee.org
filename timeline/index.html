Timeline

A glimpse of all splendid events by IEEE Student Branch of College of Engineering Chengannur.

  • 1996

    Formed IEEE Student Branch

  • 1997

    Became Third Largest Student Branch in Region 10

  • 1998

    IEEE Student Branch Library

    Established IEEE Student Branch Library with 39 journals and 23 transactions.

  • 1998

    First prize in IEEE product design contest

    SB had won the first prize in IEEE Product Design Contest held at Technopark, Thiruvananthapuram.

  • 1999

    Conducted ELIXIR'99 -All Kerala Student Congress.

  • 2000

    Awarded Best Student Branch in Kerala Section

  • 2000

    Organized SUMMIT 2000 (South India Technical Symposium).

  • 2000

    Organized ELIXIR 2000 (All Kerala Student Congress).

  • 2001

    Conducted SPAVE 2001 (Student Professional Awareness Venture).

  • 2001

    Student members attended IEEE National Congress and Technical Symposium.

  • 2002

    Region 10 Student Congress was attended by Dipu Jose, Vice-Chairman in Singapore.

  • 2002

    Organised SUMMIT 2002(South India Technical Symposium).

  • 2003

    IEEE Student Branch website won the fifth prize in Region 10.

  • 2003

    IEEE Student Branch received the certificate of exemplary work.

  • 2003

    The launch of IEEE Robotics Initiative.

  • 2003

    Organized STAC 2003(Student Technical Awareness Congress).

  • 2004

    Formation of IEEE Women In Engineering Affinity Group

    Formed IEEE Women In Engineering Affinity Group for the first time in Region 10.

  • 2004

    Center Of Excellence Award

    Received The Center Of Excellence Award for the first time in Kerala and third time in Asia Pacific region.

  • 2004

    Holding the post of Student Representative to the Executive Committee of the Kerala section for the past several years.

  • 2004

    Three students from the college were chosen to represent the Kerala section for the Region 10 Student Congress held at Hong-Kong in July 2004.

  • 2005

    Prijoe Philips Komattu, Chairman of the Student Branch was awarded the “Larry K. Wilson Award"

  • 2005

    The WIE Affinity Group received the “Chapter of the year” award.

  • 2005

    Organised Summit 2005 - South India Level SPAC with participation from 33 institutions

  • 2006

    Nisha Kuruvilla, SB Counselor was awarded Outstanding Branch Counselor Award by IEEE Headquarters.

  • 2006

    Awarded Best SB award of IEEE Kerala Section.

  • 2006

    Jaison Abey Sabu awarded Best student volunteer award of IEEE Kerala Section.

  • 2006

    Started an IEEE Computer Society Club.

  • 2006

    Organized LINK camp

  • 2007

    Anoop Mammen was awarded the best student volunteer award of IEEE Kerala Section.

  • 2007

    Shaun Thomas, Achal Sreedharan and Suraj Sebastian won first prize in student ethic competition sponsored by the Ethics and Member Conduct Committee of IEEE Headquarters.

  • 2007

    Conducted Elixir’07, a south India level technical-cum-management festival.

  • 2007

    WIE affinity group conducted a STAR program at Bishop Hodges Higher Secondary School, Mavelikkara.

  • 2008

    6 students from the college were selected to attend Region 10 student congress at SSN college of engineering, Chennai.

  • 2008

    Organized Hub2 Student Congress.

    Initiated IGNITE, a school outreach program, and the first phase was implemented successfully across Kerala Section.

  • 2013

    Darrel Chong Outstanding Student Activity Award in Silver category, for IGNITE
